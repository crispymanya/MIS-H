    Vision & Mission

    To be one of the leading K-12 (Kindergarten to Grade 12) school, opening path of self-discovery for the students by creating a truly exceptional school which inspires, empowers and enlightens students and enriches the communities that we are part of.

    To foster emergence of responsible learners into the world, by providing best quality education; with comprehensive curriculum and innovative learning.

    Our Motto

    Developing Global Citizens

    The 21st century has presented new opportunities & challenges which require a different approach to education. In fast-changing & interdependent world, it is necessary for young people to acquire knowledge, skills, values & attitudes to adapt and to thrive.

    Our education should prepare students for living and working in a global society. This is why developing global citizens is the key.


    Overview

    Our Education System Inspires You More.

    MIS International School, Hinjawadi, is a coeducational, private, progressive, creative, child-centred, and English-medium school dedicated to providing every student with a top-notch education. The school offers Pre-Primary, Primary, Secondary, and Higher Secondary education following the CBSE curriculum. We strive to provide exceptional educational programs, focusing on developing creative and stimulating learning environments. To further enhance experiential learning, the school features State-of-The-Art Infrastructure, STEAM Labs, Coding Lab, Well Stocked Library, Performing Art Studios, Sports Education Facilities, and Smart Classrooms. Our mission at MIS International School is to provide affordable, comprehensive education emphasizing global citizenship, preparing students for the challenges of tomorrow.

    Pedagogical Approach



    Pre-Primary

    We create a safe, joyful, child-centric environment that builds curiosity through hands-on exploration. Emphasis is on language, social habits, early numeracy–literacy, motor skills and values to ensure a smooth transition to primary schooling.

    • Play-based, activity-rich learning
    • Focus on socio-emotional growth
    • Development of fine & gross motor skills

    Primary

    Conceptual understanding is built through integrated projects and experiential tasks. We nurture reading, writing, logical thinking and collaborative work while strengthening communication and creativity.

    • Concept-led, multi-disciplinary projects
    • Reading & language development
    • Foundations in Math & Science

    Middle & Secondary

    A STEAM-aligned program that bridges ‘syllabus’ and ‘skill’. Learners engage in problem-solving, research, design thinking and real-life application, preparing them for higher studies and future careers.

    • Inquiry, research & presentation skills
    • Technology integration & labs
    • Career-ready competencies
