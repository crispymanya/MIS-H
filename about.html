    Overview

    Our Education System Inspires You More.

    MIS International School, Hinjawadi, is a coeducational, private, progressive, creative, child-centred, and English-medium school dedicated to providing every student with a top-notch education. The school offers Pre-Primary, Primary, Secondary, and Higher Secondary education following the CBSE curriculum. We strive to provide exceptional educational programs, focusing on developing creative and stimulating learning environments. To further enhance experiential learning, the school features State-of-The-Art Infrastructure, STEAM Labs, Coding Lab, Well Stocked Library, Performing Art Studios, Sports Education Facilities, and Smart Classrooms. Our mission at MIS International School is to provide affordable, comprehensive education emphasizing global citizenship, preparing students for the challenges of tomorrow.
