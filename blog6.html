    Fostering Curiosity Through Inquiry-Based Learning

    May 30, 2024

    Curiosity is the spark that ignites lifelong learning. At MIS Hinjawadi, we encourage our students to ask questions, explore, and discover through inquiry-based learning.

    What is Inquiry-Based Learning?

    Inquiry-based learning places students at the center of the learning process. Instead of passively receiving information, students actively seek answers, analyze data, and construct new understanding.

    • Promotes higher-order thinking and problem-solving skills
    • Encourages independent research and collaboration
    • Fosters creativity and adaptability
    • Develops a growth mindset and love for learning

    How We Practice Inquiry at MIS Hinjawadi

    • Open-ended classroom questions and discussions
    • Research projects and science experiments
    • Real-life investigations and fieldwork
    • Student-led presentations and debates

    At MIS Hinjawadi, we believe curiosity is the gateway to innovation, resilience, and success in a rapidly changing world.
