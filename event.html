    MIS Events

    📸 Take a glimpse into the vibrant world of MIS Hinjawadi through our past events — celebrating learning, creativity, culture, and community spirit.

    Euphoria Annual Gathering 2024-25

    MIS Hinjawadi’s annual celebration was a vibrant display of talent, energy, and enthusiasm. Students performed captivating dances, soulful songs, and engaging skits. The event showcased the creativity and confidence of our young performers. Parents and guests were mesmerized by the efforts and preparation. It was truly a night to remember for everyone involved. 🌟 Thank you to all who made it a grand success!

    The Christmas Carnival

    Joy, laughter, and festive cheer filled the MIS campus on 14th December 2024. Students enjoyed exciting games, delicious treats, and creative activities. The stage came alive with colorful dance performances and Christmas decorations. Parents joined in the celebration, adding warmth and love to the event. The entire school celebrated together, making it a magical memory.

    Grand Love Fest

    Our grandparents joined us for a heartwarming day of celebration and connection. The event featured interactive games, art activities, and musical performances. Students shared stories, danced with their elders, and created joyful moments. This special day fostered respect, gratitude, and the true spirit of giving. It reminded us of the precious bond between generations.

    S.A.F.E (Students and Faculty Empowered)

    A unique safety-awareness event to build trust between students and community helpers. Children interacted with police officers and learned about safety measures. They explored real-world topics like emergency response and public service. Hands-on activities made the session fun and educational. The initiative encouraged responsible behavior and mutual respect.

    Share & Shine Competition

    A stage for our little stars to express themselves with pride and excitement. Students performed dances, recited poems, and showcased art and drama. The event celebrated individuality, creativity, and confidence. Parents cheered as their children shone brightly on stage. Every child received appreciation for their unique contribution.

    Independence Day Celebration

    MIS Hinjawadi proudly celebrated India’s freedom with patriotic fervor. Children dressed in traditional attire and participated in cultural activities. The program included national songs, skits, and speeches. It helped instill respect and love for our nation in young hearts. A colorful tribute to the spirit of unity and pride.

    My Safe Circle Activity

    MIS Hinjawadi organized a special session to teach students about personal safety. Children learned the difference between good touch and bad touch through engaging activities. Interactive games and visuals made the session fun and memorable. The activity helped build confidence and awareness among young learners.

    Rainy Season Celebration

    MIS Hinjawadi students celebrated the monsoon with colorful raincoats and cheerful spirits. Fun activities like singing, dancing, and crafting brought joy to the day. Children expressed their creativity through rain-themed art and games.The event helped them appreciate nature while having fun with friends. Laughter and excitement filled the campus, making it a memorable celebration.
