    General Admission Policy

    • Admission is open to all children irrespective of race, nationality, religion, caste or gender.
    • Siblings will only receive a concession on the annual fee if they are directly connected.
    • The child must meet the age requirements that have been established and approved by the school’s managing committee and government.
    • Foreign nationals must get the necessary visa or resident permission and present the required paperwork when applying for entry.
    • The registration form is only eligible for the current academic year.
    • A written Ability test will be conducted for Grade III & above.
    • Children with special learning needs, abilities and challenges will be discussed on a case to case basis.
    • A one-on-one evaluation to determine the child’s preparedness for the class may be conducted. If the child fails the level of readiness assessment (because of fear, phobia, or known trauma from prior school experiences). A decision will then be made in accordance with the results.
    • Family meet: This is an essential stage in the procedure. There is no final interview for the parents. However, this is also a time for the parents and school administration to get to know one another, clear up misunderstandings, and ask questions.
