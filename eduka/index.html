    About Us

    Our Edukation System Inspires You More.

    MIS International School, Hinjawadi is a NEP empowered; coeducational, private, progressive, creative, child-centred, and English-medium school that is committed to providing all students with a top-notch education. The school offers pre-primary, primary, secondary and higher secondary education and follows the CBSE curriculum. The school aims to provide finest education and life skills with a focus on creating innovative and stimulating learning environment. Our goal is to offer affordable global education combined with holistic development of students. It has State-of-The-Art Infrastructure, STEAM Labs, Coding Lab, Well Stocked Library, Performing Art Studios, Sports Education Facilities, and Smart Classrooms. The planned campus will offer the conducive environment for students to learn and develop themselves into confident individuals. Our top priority is the safety of our children.
