    Our Curriculum

    MIS International School, Hinjawadi is a NEP empowered school following CBSE pattern which strives for the holistic development of the child and equips them with the necessary tools to become self-sufficient, self-assured, and prepared for the outside world! Our curriculum not only covers the required subjects but also helps child build survival skills!

    Nursery to Grade II
    (Age 3-8)

    Foundation

    • Positive Learning Habits: Builds confidence and discipline for future learning.
    • Foundational Literacy & Numeracy: Strengthens reading, writing, and math for lifelong growth.
    • Cognitive Development: Enhances critical thinking, problem-solving, and curiosity.
    • Cultural Development: Encourages respect for diverse traditions and artistic values.
    Grade III to V
    (Age 8-11)

    Preparatory

    • Prevocational Skills: Provides practical exposure to prepare for academics and careers.
    • Language Education: Improves communication through multilingual learning.
    • Activity-Based Pedagogy: Uses interactive exercises to spark curiosity and comprehension.
    • Application-Based Activities: Connects classroom learning with real-world projects.
    Grade VI to VIII
    (Age 11-14)

    Middle School

    • Conceptual Framework: Links and explains ideas across multiple subjects.
    • Nurturing Self-Discovery: Encourages students to explore strengths and passions.
    • Critical Thinking: Develops analytical skills to solve complex problems.
    • Interdisciplinary Learning: Integrates skills and knowledge across disciplines.

    At MIS International School, Hinjawadi we use the STEAM approach to teach the Theory of Multiple Intelligences,
    as presented by Prof. Howard Gardner. This helps every learner absorb knowledge in the way that suits them best.

    Conceptual Comprehension: Retaining ideas for longer periods.
    Experiential Learning: Applying ideas in practical situations.
    Communication Skills: Mastering effective communication.

    The Child Undergoes 5 A's Of A Classroom Experience

    AIM

    Direction & purpose, laying out expectations.

    ANALYSIS

    Pre-assessment activities to tailor instruction.

    ACTION

    Discussions, hands-on exercises & group work.

    ASSESSMENT

    Quizzes, projects & feedback to measure learning.

    APPLICATION

    Bridging theory & practice to show real-world relevance.
