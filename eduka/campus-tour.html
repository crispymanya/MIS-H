    Details About How To Visit Our MIS HINJEWADI

    We welcome you to experience the vibrant atmosphere and future-ready infrastructure of MIS International School, Hinjawadi. Our thoughtfully designed campus is built to inspire curiosity, foster creativity, and support every child's unique learning journey. From well-ventilated classrooms and modern science labs to engaging activity zones and safe outdoor spaces, MIS is more than just a school — it’s a second home for our students.

    A campus visit gives you the opportunity to meet our dedicated faculty, understand our teaching approach, and witness firsthand how we combine academic excellence with holistic development. Whether you’re a parent exploring schooling options or a curious visitor, we invite you to step in and see what makes MIS truly special.

    Special Campus Tour

    We warmly welcome parents and students to experience the vibrant campus life at MIS Hinjawadi. Our guided campus tours provide a glimpse into our modern classrooms, well-equipped labs, creative learning zones, activity centers, and child-friendly infrastructure. During the visit, you’ll get an opportunity to interact with faculty members, see ongoing sessions, and explore how we blend academics with creativity and discipline.

    Whether you're a prospective parent or simply curious, a campus visit is the best way to understand our unique learning environment. Schedule your tour and witness firsthand how MIS nurtures future-ready global citizens.

    Campus Tour Time And Your Tour

    We are open for campus tours from Monday to Friday, between 10 AM and 4 PM (excluding holidays).

    Experience the MIS Hinjawadi campus through a guided tour and see how we blend academics with creativity, safety, and holistic learning. Our dedicated team will walk you through our facilities and answer any queries you may have.

    • The curriculum and teaching methodology
    • Classrooms, library, labs, and play zones
    • Co-curricular and extra-curricular activities
    • Health, safety, and transport facilities
